In Hounslow, we are currently working with residents to raise Let's Raise Air-wareness amongst parents whose children are enrolled in local primary schools. We want to foster a sense of togetherness amongst this community and highlight our support in withstanding the effects of air pollution. You are not alone.  

We have launched our school-led approach in three different primary schools: 

- Fairholme Primary School 

- Green Dragon Primary School 

- St. Mary’s Catholic Primary School 

Our fantastic collaboration with these schools has raised Let's Raise Air-wareness amongst children and teachers. We encourage children to discuss what they have learned about air pollution at home with parents and guardians.
