The central ethos of Raising Airwareness is making dealing with air pollution easier. We have found Hounslow-specific support or advocacy groups that you can get involved in: 

Hounslow Borough Friends of the Earth  

As a local action group, Hounslow Borough Friends of the Earth are a part of a large network working to bring systemic change together.  

Hounslow Borough Respiratory Support Group  

This charity exists to support holistic support to residents who have been diagnosed with a long-term respiratory condition and their parents/ carers.
