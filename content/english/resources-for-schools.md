### Aimed At Teachers

Air is Kin - an environmental health justice project by Centric Lab - is collaborating with primary schools as part of the Let’s Raise Air-wareness campaign. AiK has designed resources that can be used in your school in Hounslow to help spark conversations at home about how families can protect themselves against air pollution. The resources have recieved great feedback from parents have fed back to teachers about how great it is that this campaign is supporting them an issue that can often feel solution-less.

#### Why It Matters
Hounslow is one of London’s most air-polluted areas. 
Exposure is linked to:
- Asthma & respiratory conditions
- Cardiovascular problems
- Fatigue & difficulty concentrating
- Long-term impacts on children’s development
Many families don’t connect these symptoms to air quality. The campaign helps them recognise the signs and visit their GP if concerned.

#### How Your School Can Help
This is a call to action, not just an information  campaign. We want to give families the tools to make informed, proactive decisions about their health Schools are trusted community hubs. By sharing campaign materials, you help reach the families who need this information most.
- Share take-home resources with parents & carers
- Display campaign materials in school
- Explore the classroom activities on our website

#### NEW: Homework Activity Sheet — Become an Air Detective!
A fun, curriculum-linked activity sheet for curious primary school children to explore air quality in their own neighbourhood. Pupils investigate what’s in the air around them, spot sources of pollution, and bring their findings home to share with their families - turning learning into real-life conversations. Available to download from the campaign website below.

Classroom resources, the ‘Become an Air Detective’ sheet,  and further information are all available at:shweatanhegde.github.io/air-is-kin-v2

Thank you for helping Hounslow breathe easier.
Together we can make the invisible visible. 
The Let’s Raise Air-wareness Team.

### Aimed at Parents
Dear Parents and Carers,
We have partnered with Air is Kin in their 'Let's Raise Air-wareness' campaign. This month, your children have become air detectives, noticing what they see around them and what air pollution may look like in Hounslow. Have a look at their Air Detectives worksheets and check out the campaign, which aims to make parents aware of how they can help their families withstand air pollution in Hounslow.

#### What is Air Pollution?
Air pollution refers to harmful particles and gases in the air we breathe - from traffic, industry, and other everyday sources. Hounslow is one of the 
areas in London most affected by poor air quality.
Common symptoms of exposure include:
- Persistent cough or wheezing
- Itchy or sore eyes
- Headaches and fatigue
- Worsening asthma or breathing difficulties
If you recognise any of these signs in yourself or  your child, please [speak to your GP](https://www.thecentriclab.com/air-is-kin/approach-gp-ap-childhealth).

#### About Air is Kin
Air is Kin is an environmental health justice project developed by Centric Lab. It connects communities with the knowledge and tools to protect their health from air pollution.
The campaign aims to:
- Raise awareness of air pollution and its effects on health
- Give families practical tools to protect themselves
- Encourage communities to take informed, proactive action
- Champion environmental health justice in Hounslow and beyond

#### What Can You Do?
- Check Air Quality: Look up daily air quality forecasts for Hounslow before planning outdoor activities, especially on busy roads.
- Choose Cleaner Routes: Walk your child to school on quieter back streets away from heavy traffic where possible.
- Ventilate Wisely: Open windows at home during low-traffic hours and keep them 
closed during rush hour or high pollution alerts.

#### Get Involved Today!
Find out how you can get involved in the campaign here: https://shweatanhegde.github.io/air-is-kin-v2/
