Let's Raise Air-wareness connects parents and carers with Air is Kin – an environmental health justice project centred around air pollution and developed by Centric Lab – through trusted school channels and existing community networks.  

Our intention is to raise awareness about air pollution and the adverse health effects that come from regular exposure. We want you to recognise patterns about the symptoms of air pollution exposure, encouraging you to visit your GPs if you recognise the signs.  
 
The campaign is a call to action, rather than simply being a pool of information, we want to provide you with the tools to make informed and proactive decisions about your and your child’s health.  

The campaign supports Air is Kin's vision of communities sharing knowledge and supporting one another through an "each one, teach one" model.  

Success is not simply awareness, but creating pathways through which families can share knowledge, support each other and access Air is Kin's resources through existing community networks.  
 
We are about encouraging community advocacy amongst parents – aiming for collaboration and conversation, justice and ultimately a deeper understanding of air pollution.  

Our core message is: Notice, Talk, Share – small but significant observations can start important conversations.  

Together we can make the invisible visible.
